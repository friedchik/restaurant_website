About Us

Welcome to Salt & Shore, where the ocean meets exceptional cuisine. Located just steps from Bondi Beach, we serve fresh, locally-sourced seafood and contemporary Australian dishes with a Mediterranean twist.

Our passionate team of chefs creates memorable dining experiences that capture the essence of coastal living. Whether you're watching the sunrise with our signature breakfast bowls or enjoying sunset cocktails on our terrace, every moment at Salt & Shore is designed to inspire.
